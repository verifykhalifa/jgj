EDGE BINDING

The machine is equipped with preliminary milling units to smooth the veneer surface before edge banding, which results in extraordinary quality of the veneer surface and no chipping of the board along the edge of the contact with the veneer. The material is scrapped at the end of the process to make sure the edges are smooth.

At JGJ Global Services Ltd, we use really good material called edge banding to make our displays look great and be strong enough to handle being touched by customers at stores. We can choose from lots of different colors and designs to make them look really cool!.
